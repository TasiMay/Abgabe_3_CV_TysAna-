About Us

Hello there o/

We are a casual social guild. Our focus is to built friendships and have a good time. Our members are a mature bunch of people with several nationalities and in different parts of their lifes. We are fathers, workers, students and some of us are even above 50. What brings us together is a bit of wow in the evenings.

Our guildname was created to participate in the Taliesin and Evitel challenge that is taking place each patch. Every guild with a name a bit like Immortalis can participate. Our guild was founded in the beginning of Shadowlands. Our goal each patch is to get Ahead of the Curve. That is a goal we reached ever since.

We want to raid in a fun casual environment. Our players come from different backgrounds being wow veterans and new players.

We are raiding in a fun and casual environment. We mainly focus on Raiding and Mythic+. We are hosting multiple events such as transmog runs, achievements runs, mini game nights and many more.
